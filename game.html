Memory Motorcycles

Game Rules

  • Test your memory with 20 levels of increasing difficulty
  • Each level shows a number for exactly 5 seconds - memorize it!
  • After the timer ends, enter the number you remember
  • Level 1: 1-digit number, Level 2: 2-digit number, up to Level 20: 20-digit number
  • Scoring: Level 1 = 5 points, Level 2 = 10 points, ..., Level 20 = 100 points
  • Total possible score: 1050 points
  • 840+ points = Excellent! The higher your score, the better your memory
