Memory Test

Challenge your cognitive abilities with our scientifically designed memory game. Test your short-term memory with 20 progressive levels, each increasing in difficulty. Improve your focus, concentration, and recall skills in a fun and engaging way.

Game Rules

  • 20 progressive levels with increasing number of digits
  • Each level shows a random number for 5 seconds
  • After 5 seconds, input the number you saw
  • Correct answers earn points based on the level
  • Each level has a 5-second time limit
  • Progress through all 20 levels to complete the challenge

Scoring System

  • Level 1: 5 points (1-digit number)
  • Level 2: 10 points (2-digit number)
  • Level 3: 15 points (3-digit number)
  • ...
  • Level 20: 100 points (20-digit number)
  • Total possible score: 1050 points
  • Score categories:
    • 840+ points: Excellent
    • 630-839 points: Good
    • Below 630 points: Keep Practicing

How to Play

  1. Click the "Start Challenge" button to begin
  2. Watch the number displayed for exactly 5 seconds
  3. After the number disappears, enter it in the input field
  4. Click "Submit" to check your answer
  5. Progress through all 20 levels
  6. See your final score and performance category
  7. Challenge yourself to beat your previous score!
